About Deepfake Detection

Learn how AI technology helps detect deepfake images.

Deepfakes are artificially manipulated media generated using deep learning techniques. While deepfake technology can be used for creative applications, it also poses significant risks such as misinformation and fraud.

Our Deepfake Detector uses advanced AI models to analyze images and determine their authenticity. By leveraging neural networks, the system can distinguish between real and fake images with high accuracy.

This project is powered by EfficientNet, a state-of-the-art deep learning model optimized for image classification tasks. The model has been fine-tuned on deepfake datasets to provide reliable detection results.
